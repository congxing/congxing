# Medium Bio

Congxing Cai is an AI founder-operator and Partner/CTO at Embedding VC. Previously, he led engineering for Snap Discover & Stories, scaling personalized content systems to 200M+ daily viewers with an 80+ person org. Earlier, he worked on ads and monetization systems at Google and built his technical foundation in NLP, ranking, and information retrieval through research at USC/ISI, Penn State, and MSRA. He later founded Youpi, raising from top investors and building a 12-person team to launch 10+ AI consumer products including Realm AI and Synco AI. He now helps technical founders build products, teams, and companies.
