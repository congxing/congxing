# Medium Bio

Congxing Cai is an AI founder-operator and Partner/CTO at Embedding VC. Previously, he led engineering for Snap Discover & Stories, worked on ads and monetization systems at Google, and built his technical foundation in NLP, ranking, and information retrieval through research at USC/ISI, Penn State, and MSRA.

He later founded Youpi, building AI-native products including Realm AI and Synco AI, and now helps technical founders build products, teams, and companies.
