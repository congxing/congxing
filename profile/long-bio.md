# Long Bio

Congxing Cai is an AI founder-operator and Partner/CTO at Embedding VC, where he helps technical founders build AI-native products, teams, and companies.

Previously, Congxing led engineering for Snap Discover & Stories, working across personalized consumer systems, recommendation infrastructure, mobile/web products, moderation workflows, and large engineering teams. Earlier, he worked on ads and monetization systems at Google, leading product launches and building scalable, low-latency systems.

Before his industry work, Congxing built his foundation in NLP, ranking, information retrieval, and social media search through research at USC/ISI, Penn State, and MSRA.

Most recently, he founded Youpi, where he built AI-native consumer products including Realm AI, a multimodal social creation app, and Synco AI, a real-time AI copilot for interviews and live conversations. That journey gave him hands-on lessons across product strategy, ML systems, GTM, pricing, hiring, fundraising, and founder psychology.

Today, his work spans AI investing, venture studio building, recruiting intelligence, data/evaluation workflows, GenAI creative tools, Physical AI, LLM foundations, and founder support.
