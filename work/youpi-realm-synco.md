# Youpi / Realm AI / Synco AI

Founded Youpi to explore AI-native consumer products across creation, communication, and live assistance.

## Realm AI

Realm AI was a multimodal social creation app for generating and sharing AI-powered visual stories with friends.

The product explored how generative models could help people create scenes, stories, and imagined moments beyond what cameras capture.

## Synco AI

Synco AI was a real-time AI copilot combining streaming transcription and LLM reasoning to assist users during interviews and live conversations.

The product explored how AI could support users in high-pressure live moments by listening, summarizing, reasoning, and suggesting helpful responses.

## Founder lessons

The Youpi journey produced practical lessons across:

- AI product-market fit
- multimodal generation systems
- real-time LLM product design
- latency and cost tradeoffs
- pricing and packaging
- user acquisition
- founder-led hiring
- GTM experimentation
