# Microsoft Research Asia

Research Internship, Microsoft Research Asia
October 2005 - July 2006 | Beijing

## Context

Research internship at MSRA focused on document retrieval and learning to rank.

## What I worked on

- Document retrieval systems and ranking algorithms
- Learning to rank approaches for search and information retrieval
- Early exposure to large-scale ML research culture and methodology

## Why it matters now

MSRA was the earliest serious research experience. Working on learning to rank and document retrieval at one of the world's top research labs planted the seeds for my later work in recommendation systems at Snap, search at USC/ISI, and the ML-systems intuition I bring to evaluating AI products today.
